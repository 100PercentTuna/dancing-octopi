    <h1 class="hero-title" data-reveal="up">Hi, I'm <span class="name">Kunaal</span></h1>
    <p class="hero-intro" data-reveal="up">
        I am drawn to patterns and sense-making via systems. My day job requires simplifying complexity; here I do the opposite: I look for complexity in things that pretend to be simple. I read geopolitics, military history, grand strategy, psychology, and anthropology – with a single throughline: how human collectives rise, ossify, adapt, and collapse.<br />
In a different life, I’d be an academic or a diplomat. In this one, I keep lists of the best pizza, ramen, and hole-in-the-wall restaurants across dozens of cities, watch UCLA lose football games at 3 a.m. from the other side of the planet, and irreverently debate irrelevant minutiae over an irresponsible number of beers.<br />
This blog is where I plan to work through half-formed ideas: the ones I’m still figuring out, and the ones I’ll probably abandon before they harden into anything resembling a conclusion. <span class="hand-note">(almost certainly)</span>
